Clubs

We have many clubs to offer here at Hadyn King Memorial Academy. Our student's extracurriculars are very importnat to us. Here are some of the clubs we offer:

Chess Club

Join the Chess Club to challenge your mind and improve your strategic thinking skills. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, our club welcomes all skill levels. We meet every Wednesday after school in Room 101. Come and make new friends while enjoying the game of chess!

Drama Club

Unleash your inner actor in the Drama Club! We offer opportunities to participate in plays, improv sessions, and scriptwriting workshops. Our club meets every Tuesday and Thursday in the auditorium. No prior experience is necessary, just bring your enthusiasm and creativity!

Science Club

Explore the wonders of science in our Science Club! We conduct exciting experiments, discuss recent scientific discoveries, and even organize field trips to local science museums. Meetings are held every Friday in the science lab. Join us to fuel your curiosity and passion for science!

Art Club

Express yourself in the Art Club! We provide a space for students to explore various art forms, including painting, drawing, and sculpture. Our club meets every Monday in the art room. All skill levels are welcome, so come and let your creativity shine!

Music Club

Join the Music Club to share your love of music! We offer opportunities to perform, compose, and learn about different musical genres. Meetings are held every Wednesday in the music room. Whether you play an instrument or just love to sing, there's a place for you in our club!
